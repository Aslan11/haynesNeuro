Welcome!

Haynes Neurosurgical is a medical practice that specializes in neurological surgery. We treat diseases of and traumas to the nervous system, which encompasses the brain, spine, nerve and arteries associated with carrying blood to the brain. Our primary office is located at Birmingham Baptist Medical Center Princeton, but we also maintain a clinic at UAB Medical Center West. Thank you for selecting our office to provide the medical services you require. We will provide you with quality care in a very professional, efficient, and caring manner. Please take a few moments to read our web site, as it provides very informative and useful information. We have provided information on our physicians, surgical care, our policies and procedures, frequently asked questions, and much more.
